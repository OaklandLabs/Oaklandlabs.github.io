Delete Account or Data

Provenant by Oakland Labs — Last Updated: May 2026

Permanent

Delete Your Account

Deleting your account permanently removes your Provenant account and all associated cloud data. This action cannot be undone.

How to delete your account in the app:

  1. Open the Provenant app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (gear icon).
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the settings screen.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What is deleted:

Data Outcome Retention
Your Provenant account & email Permanently deleted None
Collection items & item details Permanently deleted None
Item photos Permanently deleted None
Wishlist items Permanently deleted None
Collection stats & value history Permanently deleted None
Goals Permanently deleted None
Local data on your device Permanently deleted None
Subscription / billing history Managed by Apple / Google — not deleted by us Per Apple / Google policy
Can't access the app? Email us at support@oaklandlabs.app and we will delete your account manually within 30 days.
Keeps Account

Delete Your Data (Without Deleting Your Account)

You can erase all of your cloud-stored collection data while keeping your account active. Your account credentials are preserved so you can continue using the app or start a fresh collection.

How to delete your cloud data in the app:

  1. Open the Provenant app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (gear icon).
  3. Tap Delete Cloud Data.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What is deleted vs. kept:

Data Outcome Retention
Collection items & item details (cloud) Permanently deleted from cloud None
Item photos (cloud) Permanently deleted from cloud None
Wishlist items (cloud) Permanently deleted from cloud None
Collection stats & value history (cloud) Permanently deleted from cloud None
Your Provenant account & email Kept — account remains active Until you delete your account
Local data on your device Kept on your device Until you uninstall the app
Can't access the app? Email us at support@oaklandlabs.app and we will process your data deletion request within 30 days.
