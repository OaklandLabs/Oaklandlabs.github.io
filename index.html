About Us

Oakland Labs LLC

Oakland Labs LLC is an independent mobile software company based in the United States, founded to build precise, well-crafted apps for enthusiast communities. Our first product, Provenant, is purpose-built for sports memorabilia and autograph collectors.

We design for people who take their collections seriously — building native iOS and Android experiences that respect user privacy and put data ownership first. Your collection data is never sold or shared with advertisers.
